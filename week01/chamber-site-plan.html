Overview

Purpose

The Valera Chamber of Commerce is a non-profit association that promotes the development of the business sector in our community. Our objectives are to promote interaction between our members, encourage good relations between commercial sectors and be a platform to request collaboration from any grouping of a similar nature to the Chamber of Commerce of Valera

Audience

The audience for this website is any business or major organization interested on joining the chamber, knowing the latest news, events and members or wanting to sponsor the chamber.

Scenarios

  • What are the latest business news in the chamber?
  • How can i become a member of the chamber?
  • Who are the latest members in the chamber?
  • What are the benefits of joining the chamber?
  • What is the history of the chamber of commerce
